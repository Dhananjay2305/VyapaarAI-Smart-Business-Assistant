The Real Problem

Why Indian MSMEs Are Losing Money

India has over 6+ crore MSMEs registered under the Ministry of MSME. Most small businesses still run on guesswork, not data.

No Profit Tracking

Business owners don't know their exact profit. Expenses, revenue, and margins are all estimated, not calculated.

No Demand Prediction

They can't predict which products will sell next week. Festivals, seasons, and trends go unanalyzed.

Manual Stock Keeping

Inventory managed in notebooks. Overstocking, understocking, and expired goods cause daily losses.

Risky Credit Sales

Goods sold on credit without any risk analysis. Customers delay payments, causing cash flow crises.

Costly ERP Software

Enterprise tools cost ₹10,000+/month — completely out of reach for small kirana shops and traders.

Complex English UI

Most tools are in English with complex jargon. Tier-2 and Tier-3 city owners struggle to use them.

Real Example: A Kirana Shop Owner

Buys 50 kg rice → Sells slowly → Doesn't know why sales dropped → Loses money to expiry → Gives credit, customers delay → Result: Low profit + poor decisions.
