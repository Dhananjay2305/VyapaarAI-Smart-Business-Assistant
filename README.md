# VyapaarAI-Smart-Business-Assistant

## 🚀 Overview

VyapaarAI is an AI-powered web application designed to help small and medium businesses manage their operations smarter.
The platform uses Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to provide insights such as sales forecasting, inventory management, expense tracking, and business analytics.

Small businesses often struggle with manual record keeping and lack data-driven decision making. VyapaarAI solves this problem by providing an intelligent assistant that analyzes business data and provides actionable insights.

The goal of this project is to make advanced AI tools accessible to small business owners in a simple and affordable way.



## 🎯 Problem Statement

Millions of small businesses operate without proper digital tools. Common challenges include:

* Manual accounting and inventory tracking
* No prediction of future sales or demand
* Poor stock management
* Difficulty tracking expenses and profits
* Lack of business analytics

These issues result in inefficient operations and financial losses.

---

## 💡 Solution

VyapaarAI provides an AI-based assistant that helps businesses understand their data and make better decisions.

The system allows users to:

* Track sales and expenses
* Monitor inventory levels
* Predict future sales using machine learning
* Analyze profits and trends
* Interact with an AI assistant to ask business-related questions

---

## 🔥 Key Features

### AI Sales Forecasting

Uses machine learning models to predict future sales trends based on historical data.

### Smart Inventory Management

Tracks stock levels and alerts users when inventory is low or overstocked.

### AI Chat Business Assistant

Users can ask questions such as:

* “What is my monthly profit?”
* “Which product sells the most?”
* “Show my weekly sales report.”

### Bill Scanner (OCR)

Upload a bill image and the system extracts data such as amount, date, and product details.

### Credit Risk Prediction

Analyzes customer transaction history to predict possible late payments.
